1. Who We Are

$WATT Protocol is operated by Velion Global Technologies. We build decentralized infrastructure that rewards individuals for generating renewable energy. Our website is wattprotocol.io. Contact us at hello@wattprotocol.io.

2. What Data We Collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Email address — to send you waitlist confirmation, dashboard access, and product updates.
  • Referral code — if you were referred by another user, we record which referral code was used.
  • Signup timestamp — to determine waitlist position and founding member status.
  • IP address — collected automatically by our server for security and abuse prevention. Not stored long-term.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data at this stage.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To send your waitlist confirmation email and whitepaper.
  • To calculate your waitlist position, referral count, and $WATT token allocation.
  • To send you product updates, launch announcements, and protocol news (you can opt out at any time).
  • To prevent duplicate signups and fraudulent referrals.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU (Ireland) region. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Emails & Opt-Out

By joining the waitlist you consent to receive transactional and marketing emails from $WATT Protocol. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also unsubscribe at any time by emailing hello@wattprotocol.io with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line.

Unsubscribing removes you from our mailing list but does not delete your waitlist spot — your position is preserved.

6. Cookies

We use a single first-party cookie (watt_ref) to track referral codes for a 7-day window so that referral credit is correctly attributed if you close and reopen your browser before signing up. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking, or analytics platforms that profile you across other websites.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@wattprotocol.io. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your waitlist data for the duration of the project and for up to 2 years after the protocol launches. You may request deletion at any time.

9. Children

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the protocol develops. We will notify waitlist members by email of any material changes. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
