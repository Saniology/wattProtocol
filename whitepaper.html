Section 01

Abstract

$WATT Protocol introduces the world's first universal Energy-to-Earn ecosystem — automatically rewarding individuals for generating renewable energy using blockchain technology.

The global transition to clean energy is one of the most urgent challenges facing humanity. Despite the clear environmental imperative, ordinary individuals who install solar panels, generate wind energy, or adopt other renewable sources receive no direct financial reward for their contribution to decarbonizing the planet. Carbon credits flow to large corporations. Energy savings flow to utilities. The data generated by millions of small producers flows to governments. The individual is left out entirely.

$WATT Protocol changes this permanently. By deploying a compact IoT smart meter device alongside a transparent on-chain reward mechanism, $WATT automatically issues ERC-20 tokens to any individual or entity generating verified renewable energy — anywhere in the world, at any scale, without intermediaries.

Every kilowatt-hour of clean energy generated anywhere on Earth deserves a financial reward. $WATT makes this possible for the first time — automatically, verifiably, and without permission from any central authority.

Section 02

The Problem

Clean energy benefits the entire planet, but the people who generate it are never compensated for that global benefit. This is a fundamental market failure.

Three Structural Failures

No individual carbon market access. Carbon credit markets require scale, certification, and institutional infrastructure that ordinary people cannot access. A household cannot sell carbon offsets even if their solar panels generate verifiable clean energy data.

No verifiable individual energy data layer. There is no universal, tamper-proof system for recording and verifying individual clean energy generation at scale. Without verified data, rewards cannot be issued fairly.

No trustless reward mechanism. Even if data existed, there is no system to automatically convert that data into financial value without a centralized intermediary that can be gamed, corrupted, or exclude participants.

Section 03

The Solution

Three layers working together: a hardware device, a verification protocol, and a reward token — creating a seamless energy-to-earn experience for anyone on Earth.

1 KWh Generated = 1 $WATT Earned

Layer 1 — The WATT Smart Meter. A compact, weatherproof IoT device that attaches to any solar panel or inverter. Measures energy with Class 1 accuracy, cryptographically signs every reading, and transmits verified data to the blockchain every 15 minutes.

Layer 2 — The Verification Protocol. Smart contracts on Base (Ethereum L2) that receive signed energy data, verify cryptographic signatures, cross-reference with oracle data, and trigger automatic reward distribution. Fully transparent and open source.

Layer 3 — The $WATT Token. An ERC-20 utility token earned at 1 $WATT per verified kWh. Immediately tradeable, stakeable, and redeemable for real-world value globally.
